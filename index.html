Amazing novels presentation

The war died down between the western enlightened Loire and the dense northern Alatyr. The emperor, who lost an entire army in an impenetrable icy country, by all means intends to conclude an eternal peace. In the land of snow and mysterious magicians who turn into wild animals, Princess Gabrielle will face many dangers and mysteries.

The Moon Series is a story about ancient spirits, powerful magic and the love of those who were never destined to meet.

Book 1: Eternal ice

The star of the court ballet Gabrielle, the favorite grandchild of the Emperor of the Loire, one of the most powerful countries of the West, led a serene existence at court. But one day she is sent to a snowy wild land. A place where, according to rumors, eternal winter reigns, and uncouth barbarians ride bears.

Her destiny is to marry Tsarevich of Alatyr and become the guarantor of eternal peace between the once warring countries. Now Gabriel will either have to please the stern Tsarevna Olga and try to fall in love with a stranger, or disappear forever in a foreign country.

Book 2: The Forest of Monsters

As a result of a palace coup, Tsarevna of Alatyr was killed, and the heir to the throne Andrei, together with his bride Gabrielle, were forced to flee and hide in the waste Alatyr forests. However, the human enemies is not their only problem. The woods of this country are full of ancient spirits, dangers and such deaf and dead places where it is easy to perish.

Making their way through the dense forests, young people will have to face many dangers and trials in order to prove to the ancient land the sincerity of their feelings and find support in order to return the throne to the rightful ruler of Alatyr.

Book 3: The Troubles

To prove his right to the throne, Tsarevich Andrei has to find the ancient guardian spirit of Alatyr, an immortal bloodthirsty monster. On this dangerous journey, the heir to the throne sets off alone, sending Gabrielle to safety with friends. However, the western princess does not intend to sit idly by the window and wait for her husband to return.

The girl looks for allies among the scattered boyar estates, creates disarray among the troops of the enemies and in fact proves to the stern conservative nobility of Alatyr that she is no longer a stranger and deserves to be near the future Tsar.

Book 4: The foreign summer

Four years have passed since the events of the last book. Alatyr is slowly turning towards west, staring to technologically grow. The dynasty is now safe and acquired heirs. A golden age has come to Alatyr.

This attracts the attention of the Western countries, who invite the rulers of the Alatyr to unprecedented negotiations. The Loire, Gabrielle's homeland, is tired of waging protracted, exhausting wars and, under the roule the new Emperor, seeks to make peace with old enemies. This means that the path of young sovereigns lies to the west.
